# Vessel Surveys

Professional surveys and inspections of M/Y Becoming.

## Organization

Surveys are organized by type and date:
- `marine-survey-YYYY-MM.pdf` - Full marine survey
- `engine-survey-YYYY-MM.pdf` - Engine/mechanical survey
- `electrical-survey-YYYY-MM.pdf` - Electrical system survey
- `out-of-water-YYYY-MM.pdf` - Haul-out and bottom inspection

## Survey Types

### Marine Survey
Comprehensive inspection of the vessel's overall condition, structure, systems, and equipment. Typically required for insurance and financing.

### Engine Survey
Detailed inspection of engines, fuel systems, transmissions, and related systems. Often done separately from the general survey.

### Electrical Survey
Inspection of electrical systems, wiring, panels, and equipment.

### Out-of-Water Survey
Bottom inspection, through-hulls, running gear, rudders, and underwater components.

## Survey Reports

Survey reports should include:
- Date of survey
- Surveyor's name and credentials
- Detailed findings
- Recommendations for repairs or upgrades
- Photos and documentation

## Cross-References

Survey findings often lead to:
- **Maintenance items** → `logs/maintenance/`
- **System upgrades** → `logs/upgrades/`
- **Updated system documentation** → `systems/`

Keep surveys as historical snapshots of vessel condition. Use them to track improvements over time and plan maintenance priorities.
